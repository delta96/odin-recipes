Ingredients:

  • 8oz of Sirloin Steak
  • 1 White Onion
  • Bushel of Cilantro
  • 2 Limes
  • Tortillas
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Avocado Oil

Steps:

  1. Dry steak with paper towels
  2. Dice steak into small cubes ad place to the side
  3. Dice onions and set in a serving bowl
  4. Mince cilantro and set in a serving bowl
  5. Heat skillet on medium-high heat with oil
  6. Throw steak into hot skiller to start cooking
  7. Season generously with salt and black pepper
  8. In a separate pan start heating tortillas
  9. Once steak is golden brown with a nice seared crust, take out to rest.
  10. On a dinner place, lay out three tortillas
  11. Add steak, cilantro, and onion to the tortillas
  12. Squeeze cut limes to flavor
  13. Enjoy!

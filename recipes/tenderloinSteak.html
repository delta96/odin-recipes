Tenderloin Steak

Description

The perfect steak for a romantic dinner! With the reverse sear method, you can really reclaim control of exactly how you want to cook your steak!

Ingredients:

  • 2 fillets of tenderloin steak
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Canola oil
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Dry steak and let sit at room temp for 10 minutes
  2. Pre-Heat oven to 200 degrees fahrenheit
  3. Place steak on a baking sheet and let cook for 18-20 min (rare); 20-23 minutes (medium rare); 23-26 min (medium well)
  4. Once the oven time is up, take out steak and pat dry
  5. Season both sides of the steak with salt, black pepper, and garlic powder
  6. Let steak rest at room temp for approx. 10 minutes
  7. While you let steak rest, pre-heat skillet at medium-high heat with canola oil
  8. Once the skillet is up to heat and the 10 minutes of rest are up, throw steak on skillet; sear each side for 2 minutes
  9. When you are searing the the second side, throw butter in the skillet to baste the steak
  10. Plate steak and let sit for a couple minutes before cutting
  11. Serve and Enjoy!
